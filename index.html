Area Calculator

Information:

This Custom Calculator results in
finding the area of 4 geometric shapes:

Circle | Triangle | Square | Rectangle

Area Formulas will be shown depending on user's choice.
Input for shape(s) is drop down options.
Input for side is type number.



Formula for Circle:

A = Πr2

Formula for Triangle:

A = bh⁄2

Formula for Square:

A = s2

Formula for Rectangle:

A = lw
